How to play

The basics
Hatch one of four eggs and raise it through six life stages over about a week of real time. Stats drift down while you're away, but slowly — a check-in every day or two is plenty to keep your friend happy and healthy.

Caring for your pet
🍔
Feed — Full meal, snack, or water. Snacks boost happiness but add weight.
🎮
Play — Six mini-games (Rock Paper Scissors, Coin Flip, Tic Tac Toe, Echo Dance, Star Catch, Hi-Lo). Wins boost happiness and shape your pet's evolution.
🌫
Clean — Pets poop every few hours. Clean it up before cleanliness drops too low.
🌙
Sleep — Restores energy. Pets won't accept food or play while sleeping.
💊
Medicine — Cures sickness. Don't give it when they're healthy or they'll feel worse.
🛍
Shop — Spend coins on treats and accessories. Earn coins from mini-games, daily check-in streaks, and surprise events. Rare accessories rotate stock daily.
👉
Discipline — Pets sometimes beg, tantrum, or sulk. Scold the fakers, comfort the genuinely sad. Discipline shapes which adult form they become.
Life goes on
The world follows the real clock — sunny days, dusk, and starry nights. Sleeping at night restores extra energy. Keep an eye out for shooting stars and visiting pets. When a well-raised pet passes, its spirit blesses your next egg, and every friend is remembered in the Hall of Fame.

Stats to watch
🍔 Hunger
Drops over time — feed regularly
♥ Happy
Drops without play and snacks
☣ Clean
Drops from poops and time
⚡ Energy
Drops with activity, restored by sleep
✚ Health
Falls when other stats hit zero
Lifecycle
Egg (1h) → Baby (6h) → Child (1d) → Teen (2d) → Adult (2d) → Senior. Total: about 7 days of real time. How you care for your pet shapes which adult form it grows into — three paths per species, twelve total.
Death & new pets
Neglect leads to sickness and eventually death. When your pet passes you can hatch a new one. Past pets are remembered in Settings → Pet history.
Tip: tap the 📊 stats button to see all numbers and your care score.
